## **GANs/Autoencoders Practice**

**Objective:** apply a model based on GANs or Autoencoders to a freely chosen use case by the student (completely free theme).

---

## **Mandatory Deliverables**
- Reproducible **source code** (notebook or scripts) with execution instructions.
- **Video tutorial** (max. 10 minutes) showing:
   - the use case and data employed,
   - model architecture and flow (GAN or Autoencoder),
   - challenges and implemented solutions,
   - analysis of results and metrics,
   - proposals for improvement.

---

## **Minimum Requirements**
- Choose and justify a specific use case.
- Implement and train a model (GAN or Autoencoder) that addresses the case.
- Include data preprocessing and train/val/test split.
- Provide reproducible commands or cells to generate results.

---

## **Quick Instructions**
1. Define the use case (one sentence) and the dataset.
2. Prepare data, document shape and size.
3. Implement architecture and train the model.
4. Evaluate with relevant metrics and visual or numerical examples.
5. Record the explanatory video (≤10 min) addressing the points of the deliverables.

---

## **Evaluation Criteria**
- **Reproducibility:** easy to execute code.
- **Clarity:** clear and concise video (≤10 min).
- **Technical rigor:** model design and justification.
- **Critical analysis:** explanation of challenges, results, and improvements.

---

**Note:** the theme is free; the originality of the case and the quality of the analysis in the video will be valued.
